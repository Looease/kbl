Description

Compact Privacy Screen Specification

At the scene of an emergency, the Compact Privacy Screen protects the confidentiality of victims while guarding against gawkers, helping vehicle and foot traffic flow safely.
Unlike other screens which have to be transported on purpose built trailers, The Compact Privacy screen comes in 4 hand held carry bags that can be easily transported in the back of a Vehicle.

  • Tool less setup be one person in approximately four minutes
  • The 16' x 7' heavy-duty 500-denier polyester screen is fire-retardant, UV-resistant, and water-repellent.
  • 3 WindMaster® stands have dual springs that withstand wind and individually adjustable legs that stay stable on uneven terrain.
  • Components fold for convenient handling and storage.
  • Award Winner - MDI Compact Privacy Screen won the most Innovative Traffic Safety Product of the Year award 2017 from ATSSA, the largest USA safety Association
